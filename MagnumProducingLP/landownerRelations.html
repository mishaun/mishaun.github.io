Frequently Asked Questions

When are the revenue checks mailed?

The revenue checks are mailed on the 20th of each month. If you are scheduled to receive a check, please wait till the first week of the following month before contacting our owner relations department. Due to postal delays (holidays, weather, etc.), a check may take longer than expected to arrive.
