About Magnum Producing, LP

Magnum Producing & Operating Company was formed by Avinash C. Ahuja in January 1987, and was converted to Magnum Producing, LP in January, 2004. The initial focus of the company was to acquire oil and gas producing properties with limited exploration. In 1999, Magnum opened its office in Houston and became more active in the exploration and development of oil and gas. Over the years, Magnum has grown to a staff consisting of over 25 professionals dedicated towards the advancement of oil and gas exploration to service the growing energy needs within our communities.

Drilling Activity Our Explorationists form a very competent team which generates drilling prospects using petroleum geology, 2-D and 3-D geophysical techniques as well as petroleum engineering techniques. Magnum is actively drilling wells in South Texas and along the Texas Gulf Coast. In the last three years, Magnum participated in approximately 120 wells. Magnum drilled and operated approximately 40 wells and participate as non-operator in the other 80 wells. The depths of these wells ranged from 3,200' to 15,500'.

Magnum Producing's headquarters is located in Corpus Christi, Texas.
Magnum Producing has over 700,000 acres in BLM leases across the United States.
Magnum Producing operates over 100 active wells in the Texas Gulf Coast region.
